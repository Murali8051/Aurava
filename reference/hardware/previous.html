Previous generation Wiring boards

There are two previous generations of Wiring boards: The Wiring/WiringMini V1.0 based on the atmega128 microcontroller and the Wiring V1.1 Sparkfun board based on the atmega1281/atmega2561 microcontrollers. With 53 I/O pins, 8 analog inputs, 2 hardware serial ports, 6 PWM (analog outputs), SPI, TWI, 8 external interrupts pins, power regulation with output for 5V (and 3.3V in Wiring V1.1 Sparkfun).
